Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11.07.2026
Product: JobSieve
Website: www.jobsieve.com
Operator: Pergamon Ltd.

This Privacy Policy explains how JobSieve handles information when you visit our website, contact us, purchase the software, or use the JobSieve application.

JobSieve is designed to help users filter job listings, rate job opportunities against their CVs, and generate tailored CV and cover letter drafts. The product works with your own Google account and selected job sources, and uses AI-supported processing to help complete the workflow.

By using JobSieve or our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We May Collect

We may collect or process the following categories of information depending on how you use JobSieve.

Website and contact information
When you contact us through the website form, we may collect your name, your email address, the content of your message, and basic technical information necessary to receive and respond to your request.

We use this information only to respond to your message, provide support, and manage customer communication.

Purchase and transaction information
When you purchase JobSieve, payment may be processed by a third-party payment provider. We do not store your full payment card details. Depending on the payment provider, we may receive limited transaction information such as your name or email address, payment status, product purchased, transaction reference, and invoice or tax-related information where required.

Payment providers process payment data under their own privacy policies and legal obligations.

JobSieve application data
When you use the JobSieve application, you may choose to provide or connect your Google account access, Gmail job alert emails under a label such as “Jobs”, job listing content from selected job platforms, your CV files, your cover letter files, your search rules, target roles, locations, languages and job types, your scoring preferences and writing focus, and generated Excel, CV and cover letter outputs.

JobSieve uses this information to perform the workflow requested by you: filtering jobs, rating job matches, and generating tailored application drafts.

2. Google Account and Gmail Access

JobSieve works through your own Google account. The application may use Gmail job alerts as starting points to access related job platforms and scan available listings based on your selected workflow.

JobSieve does not ask you to share your Google password with us. Where Google authentication or authorization is used, access is handled through Google’s own systems. Your Google credentials are not sold, rented, or shared by us with third parties.

JobSieve uses Google account access only to provide the user-facing features you request, such as reading job alert emails under the relevant label and creating workflow outputs. We do not use Google user data for advertising, retargeting, creditworthiness assessment, or unrelated analytics.

3. AI Processing and Gemini

JobSieve uses Gemini to compare filtered job listings with your CV and to support the generation of tailored CV and cover letter drafts.

When you use AI-powered features such as “Rate My Jobs” or “Rewrite My CV/CL”, the relevant job listing text, CV content, cover letter content, scoring instructions, and writing focus may be sent to Gemini or another AI service used by the application for that specific workflow.

JobSieve does not use AI to invent professional experience or add unsupported details. However, AI-generated outputs may still contain errors, omissions, unsuitable wording, or inaccurate interpretations. You are responsible for reviewing all generated CVs, cover letters, rankings, scores and other outputs before using them.

Third-party AI providers may process data according to their own terms, privacy notices and data processing rules. You should not upload unnecessary sensitive personal data or confidential information that is not needed for your application workflow.

4. How We Use Information

We use information only for legitimate product, support and business purposes, including providing JobSieve’s filtering, rating and rewriting features, generating Excel outputs, CV drafts and cover letter drafts, responding to support or contact requests, processing purchases and providing download access, improving product functionality and fixing technical issues, preventing abuse, fraud or misuse, complying with legal, tax, accounting or regulatory obligations, and enforcing our Terms of Use.

5. What We Do Not Do

We do not sell your personal data, build a commercial database from your CVs, cover letters or job applications, share your Google credentials with third parties, use your Gmail data for advertising, submit job applications on your behalf without your review and action, or guarantee employment, interviews, job offers or application success.

6. Local Files and Generated Outputs

JobSieve is designed to let you work with your own documents and generated outputs. Depending on your device and configuration, CVs, cover letters, Excel files and generated drafts may be stored locally on your computer or in folders/services you choose.

You are responsible for managing, protecting and deleting files stored on your own computer, Google account, cloud storage, or other services.

7. Data Sharing

We may share limited information only where necessary, such as with payment processors for purchases, hosting, email or form service providers for website operation and communication, Google services for Gmail and account-based workflow functionality, Gemini or other AI service providers for AI-powered rating and rewriting features, professional advisers, accountants or legal representatives where necessary, and public authorities if required by law.

We require service providers to process data only for appropriate service-related purposes where commercially and legally possible.

Typical retention periods may include contact form messages for as long as needed to respond and maintain support records, purchase and invoice records for as long as required by applicable tax, accounting and legal obligations, support correspondence for as long as needed to provide support and resolve disputes, and application workflow data generally controlled by you on your own device or connected accounts, unless a specific service provider temporarily processes it for the requested workflow.

You are responsible for keeping your Google account, device, passwords, API keys and downloaded files secure.
