How Jobsiee Works

1. Set up your job alerts
Create job alerts on platforms like LinkedIn, Indeed and Glassdoor, then collect them under a Gmail label called “Jobs”. Jobsiee uses these alerts as entry points to reach and scan relevant listings.

2. Set your search rules
Using the Jobsiee sidebar, enter your target roles and locations, choose your preferred languages and job types, then upload your CV and cover letter files. For most users, 1–3 CVs and 1–3 cover letters will be enough to create a strong application workflow.

3. Filter your jobs
When you click “Filter My Jobs”, Jobsiee connects to your Gmail, uses the alerts under your Jobs label as gateways to the related job platforms, scans accessible listings from the related platforms, filters out irrelevant results and creates a clean Excel file with jobs that match your criteria.

4. Rate your matches
When you click “Rate My Jobs”, Jobsiee uses Gemini to compare each filtered job with your CV, score its relevance and help you identify the strongest applications to prioritize.

5. Rewrite your applications
When you click “Rewrite My CV/CL”, Jobsiee asks you to choose the roles you want to apply for and set a writing focus. It then creates tailored CV and cover letter drafts based on your own documents and the selected job listing. It does not invent experience or add unsupported details; it stays faithful to your real background.
